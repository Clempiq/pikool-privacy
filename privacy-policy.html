1. Introduction

Welcome to Pikool ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile application Pikool (the "App"), available on Google Play.

By using Pikool, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

Pikool does not require you to create an account or provide personal information directly.

However, certain data may be collected automatically through third-party services integrated into the App:

  • Advertising identifier (GAID): Used by Google AdMob to serve ads.
  • Device information: Device model, OS version, language, and general location (country/region).
  • App usage data: Crash reports, performance metrics.
  • Purchase transaction data: Managed by RevenueCat to process and restore in-app purchases.

Player names entered in-game are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

5. Advertising & Your Choices

The free version of Pikool displays ads served by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalised based on your advertising identifier. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Or by purchasing the Pikool Premium upgrade, which removes all ads.

6. Children's Privacy

Pikool is a party drinking game intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention & Security

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any data collected by third-party SDKs is governed by their respective retention policies. In-game player names are stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the App.
